1) Sohan started a business with a capital of Rs. 80000. After 6 months Mohan joined as a partner by investing Rs. 65000. After one year they earned total profit Rs. 20000. What is share of Sohan in the profit?

  • A. Rs. 5222.2
  • B. Rs. 14222.2
  • C. Rs. 6222.2
  • D. Rs. 6777.7
 The answer is B. Rs. 14222.2

2) On lending a certain sum of money on C.I. one gets Rs.9050 in 2 years and Rs.9500 in 3 years. What is the rate of interest?

  • A. Rs. 8427
  • B. Rs. 1000
  • C. Rs. 1299
  • D. Rs. 6770
 
The answer is A. Rs. 8427
